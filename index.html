Selected work

Case studies

01
Ad Revenue Platform · Voiro
Scaling a Revenue Platform When Everything Was on Fire
Enterprise onboarding, broken workflows, revenue that wouldn't reconcile. Fixed it while the train was moving.
+
The reality
  • India and MENA clients live simultaneously — different pricing rules, different market expectations
  • CPM, CPD, and CPW pricing models running in parallel with no unified reconciliation layer
  • Fill rate gaps and billing errors going undetected until finance chased them
  • Sales closing deals faster than the platform could support them
What I did
  • Rebuilt line-item architecture to handle real pricing complexity
  • Added validation guardrails that caught revenue leakage before it hit finance
  • Shipped Criteo and DV360 integrations — 4x data coverage, cross-platform reconciliation
  • Replaced one-off client customisations with reusable, scalable workflows
Outcome
  • Enterprise clients onboarded on schedule across two markets
  • Manual ops load cut — team could handle more clients without scaling headcount
  • Finance got a reconciliation framework they could trust
  • Architecture scaled to new clients without rework
02
AI Product · Sales Opportunity Agent
Making Missed Revenue Visible Before It Walks Out the Door
Advertisers were churning and the sales team only found out weeks later. I built the product that caught it in real time.
+
The problem
  • Sales had no visibility into which advertisers were at risk until they'd already left
  • Revenue data siloed across ad servers and CRMs — no single source of truth
  • Missed upsell and re-engagement opportunities went undetected for weeks
  • Account managers spending time on reporting, not selling
What I did
  • Defined vision and roadmap for Voiro Genie — conversational revenue intelligence
  • Built Sales Opportunity Agent: LLM-powered weighted scoring across spend velocity, fill rate trends, and churn signals
  • Integrated with Salesforce to surface pipeline gaps automatically
  • Prototyped the dashboard with real workflows before engineering wrote a line of code
Outcome
  • Pipeline efficiency improved 30%
  • At-risk advertisers flagged automatically — not discovered in a monthly review
  • Sales team shifted time from reporting to conversations
  • Voiro moved from a reporting platform to a revenue intelligence platform
03
0 to 1 · Brand Center
Building a Self-Serve Media Buying Platform From Zero
Publishers managing direct deals over email and spreadsheets. I took Brand Center from a blank doc to a closed beta with 5 major publishers.
+
The challenge
  • No unified tool for direct media buying — every deal negotiated over email
  • Advertisers had no real-time visibility into campaign performance
  • Reporting manual, delayed by days, and frequently wrong
  • Publishers couldn't scale direct revenue without scaling ops headcount
What I did
  • Led product discovery, design, and roadmap end-to-end — no existing playbook
  • Defined both the advertiser and publisher experience from scratch
  • Prototyped key workflows and validated with beta partners before engineering built them
  • Ran closed beta with Zee5, JioCinema, Paytm, PhonePe, and Rooter
Outcome
  • Closed beta live with 5 premium publishers
  • PMF validated before full engineering build — no wasted sprints
  • Replaced manual deal workflows with a unified platform
  • Foundation for Voiro's direct ad revenue business
